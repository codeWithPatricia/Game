About the Game

Echoes of Orisha: Osun is an experimental game project inspired by the rich mythology of the Yoruba people, centered around Òṣun — goddess of water, fertility, and compassion. The player journeys through sacred natural landscapes as Osun, guided by emotional memories and echoes of the past. The game explores identity, grief, divinity, and rebirth through atmospheric environments and interactive storytelling.

Character Design

Osun

loren Osun was designed to reflect both divine grace and human emotion. Her visual style combines glowing symbols, fluid clothing, and natural textures like gold, silk, and leaves.
